Cell Line Development Service

JOINN Biologics has a team of experts in the production of antibodies and recombinant proteins using various mammalian cell expression systems. Our state of art technology platform offers highly efficient cell line development process and guaranteed quality products for clinical applications.

Cell culture and cell line development services

  • Antibody and protein production from transient and stable mammalian cell expression systems such as CHO, HEK293 and NS0
  • Cell line development from cDNA to single cell clone selection
  • Single cell clone characterization with verified high expression level and good stability
  • Cell line banking and storage
  • Fast turnaround time and expertise with rich experiences
